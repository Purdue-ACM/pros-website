okapi::OdomChassisControllerIntegrated¶

Odometry based chassis controller that moves using the V5 motor’s integrated control. Spins up a task at the default priority plus 1 for odometry when constructed.

This class uses the V5 motor’s integrated encoders.

Moves the robot around in the odom frame. Instead of telling the robot to drive forward or turn some amount, you instead tell it to drive to a specific point on the field or turn to a specific angle, relative to its starting position.
