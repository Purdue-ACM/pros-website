okapi::OdomChassisController¶

Odometry based chassis controller. Starts task at the default priority plus 1 for odometry when constructed. Moves the robot around in the odom frame. Instead of telling the robot to drive forward or turn some amount, you instead tell it to drive to a specific point on the field or turn to a specific angle relative to its starting position.

Constructor(s)¶

Prototype
OdomChassisController(const OdometryArgs &iparams, const float imoveThreshold = 10)
Parameters  
iparams Arguments for the internal Odometry.
imoveThreshold The minimum length movement. Any movements below this threshold will not be performed.
